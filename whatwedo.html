Technology Services

Our passion for science and technology does not cease at investing, we also strive to facilitate research and development (R&D), which requires a range of services and equipment to perform.

TAQNIA International provides its affiliated research centers’ R&D labs in universities and companies with technology services, including delivering of equipment, technical assistance, etc.

We are also working alongside the Saudi Arabia Research Elements Company (SARE), a collaboration between Saudi Aramco and the King Abdulaziz City for Science and Technology (KACST). SARE provides state-of-the-art supply chain management services for research materials and equipment. Striving to become a premier provider of expedient, reliable and cost effective supply chain management services for research materials and equipment, SARE enables Saudi Arabia to excel in advanced scientific research. Being able to productively and efficiently manage the supply chain of R&D facilities will enable these research centers to successfully pursue their research initiatives, as well as benefit portfolio companies which have R&D collaborations with said research centers.

Training Consultancy

U.S. companies in Saudi Arabia are required to meeting the government’s Saudization requirement by employing a certain number of Saudi nationals in their corporation...

To aid these U.S. corporations in this effort, Taqnia International offers a training consultancy service that improves the hiring process in Saudi Arabia by filtering through and selecting the top talent. As part of the service, we will also take care of the Saudi nationals’ logistics in order for them to come to the U.S. for training at the corporation’s U.S. branch.

By offering this service, we aim to make the hiring process of U.S. corporations in Saudi Arabia more efficient, as well as increasing the quality of applicant’s said corporations receive.
